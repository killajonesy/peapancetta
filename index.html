Pea and Pancetta Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Olive Oil
  • 2 cups of frozen peas (thawed to room temp)
  • 500g (or 3 packets, whatever) Pancetta
  • 1 red onion (finely diced)
  • 6 or 7 large cloves of garlic (finely diced)
  • 1 bunch of scallions (green onion) (thinly sliced)
  • 1 or 2 small containers of cherry tomatoes (quartered)
  • 1/4 cup of fresh italian parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp of basil
  • 2 tsp each of salt and pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil on medium-low
  2. Add garlic and saute until colour starts to change
  3. Add red onion and saute until soft, stirring often
  4. Add pancetta and cook or about 5-7 mins.
  5. Add peas, salt and pepper and cook until soft, stirring often
  6. Add parsley, basil, and tomatoes and cook until tomatoes are soft
